About the Department

The Computer Science Department at George Mason University is a leading center for computing education and research. Our department offers comprehensive programs that prepare students for successful careers in the rapidly evolving field of computer science and information technology.

With a faculty of renowned researchers and industry experts, we provide students with cutting-edge knowledge in areas such as software engineering, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, and human-computer interaction. Our programs emphasize both theoretical foundations and practical applications, ensuring graduates are well-equipped to tackle real-world challenges in technology and innovation.
